Welcome Fellows and Companies!

Fellows: Congratulations! You have all made it through the rounds, and are officially Hackerfellows! In the next phases you all will interview with various start-ups to find a match, complete the 5 week coding bootcamp, and then begin working for your companies. The purpose of this website is to serve as the first method interaction with the companies. You will be able to view basic information about the various companies, and vote on the 5 that you are interested in.
Companies: Thank you for participating in our annual fellowship, Hackerfellows. In the next phase you will interview with the fellows to find a good match for your startup. The purpose of this website is to serve as the first method of interaction with the fellows. You will be able to view basic information about the fellows, and vote on the 5 that you are interested conducting an interview with.
